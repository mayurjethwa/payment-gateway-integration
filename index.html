about us

what makes our coffee special?

SCF is a secular, not-for-profit organization in India, registered under the Societies’ Registration Act of 1860. Set up in 2015, the organization works for ‘the cancer and care of poor people to improve their quality of life’.

SCF envisions a society where elderly have the right to an active, healthy and dignified life. It recently became the first and only Indian organization to be honoured with the ‘UN Population Award 2020’ for its exemplary work in the field of ageing, relief efforts work during the Covid 19 pandemic
